Welcome

WolvSec is a computer security club and CTF team at the University of Michigan. We host weekly public meetings where we discuss current topics and teach foundational skills related to cybersecurity. The club is comprised of students, alumni, staff, and local professionals who are all focused on creating a friendly and collaborative learning environment for cybersecurity.
